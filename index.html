❓ How to Play

🏰 Your Mission

Defend your castle against endless waves of enemies! Survive as long as you can and beat your wave record. Adjust difficulty on the homescreen (1-10) to customize your challenge.

⚔️ Combat & Targeting

Your castle automatically attacks nearby enemies with arrows (or fireballs if upgraded). Manual Targeting: Click and hold on the arena to prioritize a target! Enemies will attack your castle when they get close - melee enemies lunge in, while archers shoot from afar.

🎁 Upgrades & Debuffs

After each wave, choose from 3 random upgrades with different rarities:

Common • Uncommon • Rare • Epic • Legendary

⚠️ Beware: 5% chance per wave to face a Debuff instead! Choose which curse affects you - sometimes the lesser evil is the best choice.

Higher rarities appear more often in later waves!

🛒 Shop & Mystery Boxes

Between waves, spend gold at the shop:

Repairs: Heal your castle for 25 HP or fully restore it (scales every 10 waves)

Mystery Box: Gamble for a random upgrade or action card! Max 3 per wave.

💀 Cursed Mystery Box: 3% chance a mystery box contains a devastating debuff instead - pure chaos!

🃏 Action Cards

Collect powerful one-time-use cards from upgrades and mystery boxes! Your deck appears at the bottom of the screen. Hover to see details, click to activate. Max 5 cards. Includes: Fireball, Chain Lightning, Gold Rush, Battle Cry, Poison Cloud, Summon Knight, and more!

👹 Enemies & Scaling

Basic: Orcs, Goblin Archers, Trolls

Advanced: Vampires (heal when attacking), Ghosts (take reduced damage), Demons (fire attacks), Necromancers (ranged magic), Golems (slow & tanky), Assassins (fast & deadly)

Horde Scaling: More enemies spawn every 5 waves

Bosses: Appear every 5 waves - very dangerous!

Difficulty Scaling: Enemies get stronger each wave based on difficulty setting!

📈 Game Progression

• Every 10 waves: Shop prices increase, scaling harder

• Every 5 waves: More enemies spawn, waves get longer

• Every 5 waves: Boss battles (especially tough!)

• Later waves: Legendary upgrades appear more frequently

🎯 Tips & Strategies

• Early game: Focus on damage upgrades to clear waves quickly

• Mid game: Balance damage with defense as enemies get tougher

• Late game: Prepare for devastating debuffs - sometimes avoiding them is worth the cost!

• Use action cards strategically in tough waves

• Higher difficulty = more challenging but potentially more rewarding!

• Boss waves (5, 10, 15...) are extra tough - stock up on repairs!
