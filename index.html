📜 Patch Notes

❓ How to Play

🏰 Your Mission

Defend your castle against endless waves of enemies! Survive as long as you can and beat your wave record.

⚔️ Combat

Your castle automatically attacks nearby enemies with arrows (or fireballs if upgraded). Enemies will attack your castle when they get close - melee enemies lunge in, while archers shoot from afar.

🎁 Upgrades

After each wave, choose from 3 random upgrades with different rarities:

Common • Uncommon • Rare • Epic • Legendary

Higher rarities appear more often in later waves!

🛒 Shop

Between waves, spend gold at the shop:

Repairs: Heal your castle for 25 HP or fully restore it

Mystery Box: Gamble for a random upgrade or action card!

🃏 Action Cards

Collect powerful one-time-use cards! Your deck appears at the bottom of the screen. Hover to see details, click to activate. Max 5 cards.

👹 Enemies

Hordes: Larger enemy groups attack each wave

Orcs: Basic melee attackers that charge your castle

Goblin Archers: Ranged attackers that shoot from distance

Trolls: Heavy hitters with lots of health

Elite variants: Faster/stronger versions appear in later waves

Bosses: Appear every 5 waves - very dangerous!

🎯 Tips

• Boss waves (5, 10, 15...) are extra tough - prepare!

• Save action cards for emergencies

• Prioritize damage early, defense later

• The mystery box can give legendary items!
