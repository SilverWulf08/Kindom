📜 Patch Notes

❓ How to Play

🏰 Your Mission

Defend your castle against endless waves of enemies! Survive as long as you can and beat your wave record. Adjust difficulty on the homescreen (1-10) to customize your challenge.

⚔️ Combat & Targeting

Your castle automatically attacks nearby enemies with arrows (or fireballs if upgraded). Manual Targeting: Click and hold on the arena to prioritize a target! Enemies will attack your castle when they get close - melee enemies lunge in, while archers shoot from afar.

🎁 Upgrades & Rarities

After each wave, choose from 3 random upgrades with different rarities:

Common • Uncommon • Rare • Epic • Legendary • Mythic

✨ Mythic: The rarest and most powerful upgrades - only obtainable from Golden Mystery Boxes!

Higher rarities appear more often in later waves!

💀 Curses & Debuffs

⚠️ Cursed Waves: After wave 5, there's a 15% chance to face a curse instead of upgrades - choose the lesser evil!

💀 Mystery Box Curse: 5% chance a mystery box contains a devastating debuff (after wave 5)

☠️ Golden Box Curse: 5% chance a golden box contains a DEVASTATING curse that can wipe upgrades or set HP to 1!

🛒 Shop & Mystery Boxes

Repairs: Heal your castle for 25 HP or fully restore it (prices scale every 10 waves)

🎁 Mystery Box: Random upgrade or action card! Max 3 per wave. 5% debuff chance after wave 5.

🌟 Golden Mystery Box: Appears on boss waves (5, 10, 15...)! Guaranteed Legendary or Mythic rewards. 20% chance for Mythic, 5% chance for devastating curse. Only 1 per boss wave!

🃏 Action Cards

Collect powerful one-time-use cards! Your deck appears at the bottom of the screen (max 6 cards). Hover to see details, click to activate during waves.

Deck Full? When your deck is full, you can swap the new card with an existing one or discard it.

Cards include: Fireball, Chain Lightning, Dragon Breath, Apocalypse, Gold Rush, Battle Cry, Poison Cloud, Summon Knight, and more!

👹 Enemies & Bosses

Basic: Orcs (melee), Goblin Archers (ranged), Trolls (tanky)

Advanced: Vampires (lifesteal), Ghosts (damage reduction), Demons (fire attacks), Necromancers (magic), Golems (slow & tanky), Assassins (fast & deadly)

🔥 Bosses: Appear every 5 waves with massive HP and damage - prepare accordingly!

Scaling: More enemies spawn every 5 waves. All enemies get stronger based on difficulty!

📈 Wave Progression

• Every 5 waves: Boss battle + Golden Mystery Box available + more enemies spawn

• Every 10 waves: Shop prices increase significantly

• First 5 waves: Protected from curses and mystery box debuffs

• Later waves: Legendary upgrades appear more frequently

🎯 Tips & Strategies

• Early game: Focus on damage upgrades - no curses in first 5 waves!

• Boss waves: Save the Golden Mystery Box for a chance at Mythic upgrades!

• Mythic hunting: Golden God doubles gold, Archmage doubles magic damage, World Ender chains explosions!

• Card management: Keep your best cards - swap out weaker ones when deck is full

• Curse strategy: Sometimes picking a lesser debuff is better than a devastating one!

• Manual targeting: Click and hold to focus dangerous enemies like Necromancers or Assassins
